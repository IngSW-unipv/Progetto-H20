something about carpool...

(also car-sharing, ride-sharing and lift-sharing) is the sharing of car journeys so that more than one person travels in a car, and prevents the need for others to have to drive to a location themselves.

How use carpool...Easy and fast!

1) Register and enjoy the discovery of a new world
2) Getting to know people has never been easier
3) Take a seat and choose whether to be a driver or a passenger
